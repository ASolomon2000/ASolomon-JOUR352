10.  Why do we use media queries?

- Media queries are used in CSS to change the look and size of a webpage on different devices. 
For example, if different people are reading the same article on a desktop computer versus a smartphone, the design on the webpage has to be responsive enough to change it's look and sizing on those different devices.
This is what using media queries does.

11. What is the main difference between a serif and a sans-serif font?

- Serif fonts have fancy tails at th end of the letters similar to the way caligraphy pens write, while sans-serif fonts do not have those fancy tails.
