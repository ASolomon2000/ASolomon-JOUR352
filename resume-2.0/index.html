Aiesha Solomon was a journalism major and creative writing minor at the University of Maryland College Park. She transferred into the Journalism major from Montgomery College in Rockville. She graduated in May 2023. She has worked for multiple school publications in her time as a journalist starting from her sophomore year of high school. She is an intern with a Laurel, MD newspaper called The Laurel Independent, which she has been working for since September 2022 to December 2022 and then from June 2023 to Present. In the future, she hopes to work as an international news reporter, writer and photojournalist.

Experience:
Intern Reporter, Writer and Photographer
Laurel Independent Newspaper
September 2022 - December 2022 and June 2023 - Present

While working for the Laurel Independent, Aiesha Solomon has learned more about meeting deadlines, researching, photojournalism, photo editing and contacting sources. Meeting deadlines was a skill she had already been taught at school, but this internship gives her a feel for what it would be like to utilize that skill in a professional environment where her work is actually going to be seen by more readers than just a professor. The pressure of meeting deadlines pushes her to make sure the writing is as best as it can be.

Freelance Reporter
University of Maryland Diamondback Newspaper
February 2021 - April 2021

When Aiesha Solomon was a freelance reporter for the Diamondback newspaper, she wrote stories for the General Assignments desk where they would give out pitches to and accept pitches from students. She wrote stories on multiple stories of differing topics, like events and technology, for the online newspaper during the Spring 2021 semester. While she was working for the Diamondback, the whole school was still in virtual mode because of COVID-19 pandemic so I did my writing, research and interviewing when the school was still in lockdown, on a tight deadline and under pressure from classes.

Senior Staff Member and Associate Editor
Montgomery College Rockville Campus Advocate Newspaper
2019 - 2020

Aiesha Solomon started as a staff writer, but got promoted to senior staff member within a few semesters. After gaining more experience, she worked with the Editor-in-Chief at the time and became the Associate Editor, the second-in-charge editor of the paper. She wrote about international news after a winter vacation trip to the Philippines until the COVID-19 pandemic started, as well as events, features and on-campus news stories.
